Chronomancer Power Boon

08/07/2020 Patch Update | Benchmarks | Builds | Raid Guides | Team Compositions | Gear Optimizer
Overview

Power Boon Chronomancer is a very versatile build. In essence, you are responsible for providing permanent Quickness to five players while an Alacrity Renegade provides permanent Alacrity to the entire squad. Quickness for the remaining five players should be covered by either an additional Power Boon Chronomancer or a Condition Quickness Firebrand.

Depending on the variation and changes to utilities, you have access to valuable utility in form of Aegis, Blocks, Boon Removal,

Distortion
, Pulls, as well as access to a high amount of CC.

Dueling Greatsword

This is the default build and should be played whenever possible. If you need access to

Temporal Curtain
or
Distortion
, refer to the other variants.

Dueling Focus

This variant is only recommended for encounters where you need

Temporal Curtain
. Unlike Greatsword, your clone and therefore Quickness generation is extremely limited and you can easily override the few clones you have. To account for that, we recommend
Soul Pastry
 and
Potent Lucent Oil
 for this variant.

Inspiration Trait Line

By not running Dueling, this variant trades a large amount of damage for utility and should only be played if that utility is truly necessary. Mainly:

  • Blurred Inscriptions
     for
    Distortion
    .
  • Warden's Feedback
     for more frequent
    Temporal Curtain
     and
    Phantasmal Warden
    .

Boon Duration

The required Boon Duration varies greatly depending on your personal and your groups skill level. While the recommended value should be sufficient for every encounter, you might still find yourself dropping boons. Don't get discouraged if this happens and simply increase your Boon Duration until you are able to maintain boons reliably. As you gain experience and become more confident, you'll be able to decrease your Boon Duration.

Split Second

Continuum Split

Phantasmal Swordsman

Chaining Abilities

Almost always queue the next ability while the current one is already casting, to not waste any time in your rotation. However, beware that

Mind Stab
,
Blurred Frenzy
,
Signet of the Ether
 and Weapon Swap will cancel the currently casting ability.

Auto Attack Chains

Always finish your auto attack chains as the majority of the damage is loaded into the final part.

During the loop, you have enough leeway in between the listed skills to finish them safely.

General Rotation

Outside of

Continuum Split
, your rotation revolves around a single ability per weapon set
Phantasmal Swordsman
 on Sword and
Tides of Time
 on Shield while casting every ability according to a priority list.

You always want to swap back to Shield once you've cast

Phantasmal Swordsman
three times outside of
Continuum Split
.

On your Shield set, cast

Tides of Time
once and swap back to your Sword set as soon as possible.

Priority List

  1. Signet of Inspiration
  2. Well of Action
  3. Phantasmal Swordsman
  4. Signet of the Ether
  5. Blurred Frenzy
  6. Illusionary Leap
  7. Phantasmal Disenchanter
  8. Tides of Time

Notes
Shatters

Make sure to always pay attention at which point you can and/or will generate clones, as that determines when you are going to shatter as well the amount of clones you shatter with.

Cast

Split Second
on Cooldown. You can slightly delay it if that allows you to cast it with more clones.

Always cast

Rewinder
with zero clones right after
Split Second
.

Make sure to cast every Shatter inside of

Continuum Split
, as they generate Quickness.

Continuum Split

Once your

Continuum Split
is available again, finish your shield rotation and return to step four of the opener. Since your
Phantasmal Swordsman
 will be still on Cooldown, cast
Phantasmal Disenchanter
 instead.

Considering that

Phantasmal Disenchanter
doesn't have an aftercast, you will have to enter
Continuum Split
 slightly before its cast finishes. Therefore you will have less time available inside of
Continuum Split
, meaning that you have to cast
Split Second
 sooner and
Blurred Frenzy
 exactly when
Phantasmal Swordsman
 finishes casting.

If you are feeling adventerous, you can cast

Illusionary Leap
and cancel most of its animation with
Signet of the Ether
 for an additonal clone.

Chaining Abilities

Almost always queue the next ability while the current one is already casting, to not waste any time in your rotation. However, beware that

Blurred Frenzy
,
Signet of the Ether
 and Weapon Swap will cancel the currently casting ability.

Auto Attack Chains

Always try to chain your abilities in a way that allows you to finish auto attack chains while respecting the priority list as the majority of the damage is loaded into the final part.

However, never massively delay abilities, especially not abilities that generate or extend boons.
